## Info
Use your music server for its intended purpose and listen to music on the go. Yuzic is a clean minimal app, designed to make your music experience flawless. Manage and play your downloaded music, listen to music offline, or use our plugins for even more.

• Switch seamlessly between Navidrome and Jellyfin
• New Text-to-Music button allows users to listen to a curated playlist for any mood
• Enriched data via Last.fm
• Download albums and playlists to listen to offline

Yuzic provides a Navidrome demo but requires a self-hosted Jellyfin or Subsonic server to function as intended.
